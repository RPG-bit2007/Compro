Why Use Compro to Compress Images?

Compro is a free, browser-based image compression tool that reduces JPEG and PNG file sizes without quality loss — instantly, privately, with no uploads required.

🎯 Compress to Exactly 50 KB

Set 50 KB as the target and Compro automatically finds the perfect quality level. Ideal for passport photos, ID uploads, job applications and government forms that require images under 50 KB.

🔒 100% Private — No Uploads

Unlike other online image compressors, Compro never sends your files to any server. All compression happens inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are yours alone.

⚡ Smart Binary-Search Algorithm

Most compressors just apply a fixed quality. Compro uses a binary-search loop to find the highest possible quality that still hits your target size — giving you the best-looking result every time.

📐 Resize Before Compressing

Set exact pixel dimensions and lock your aspect ratio — all before compressing. Fewer pixels means smaller file sizes without extra quality loss.

📱 Works on Mobile & Tablet

Fully responsive and touch-friendly. Compress images directly from your iPhone, Android phone or tablet. Install as a PWA for one-tap access and offline use — no app store needed.

📅 Free Forever, No Limits

No file size limits, no daily quotas, no watermarks, no account required. Compro is completely free for unlimited use. Compress as many images as you need, whenever you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about compressing images online.

How do I compress an image to 50KB online for free? +
Upload your JPEG or PNG to Compro, ensure the Target KB field shows 50, and click Compress. The tool automatically finds the optimal quality and resizes if needed to get your image under 50 KB — entirely in your browser, completely free with no signup.
Does compressing an image reduce its quality? +
JPEG compression always involves some quality trade-off, but Compro minimises this by using a binary-search algorithm to find the highest quality level that still meets your target file size. For most images the quality difference is barely visible at 50 KB.
Is my image uploaded to a server? +
No. Compro processes all images entirely inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your files never leave your device and are never sent to any server, making it the most private image compressor available online.
What image formats are supported? +
Compro accepts JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg) and PNG (.png) files as input. All output is saved as optimised JPEG for maximum compression. PNG files are automatically converted to JPEG during compression.
Can I compress images on my phone? +
Yes. Compro is fully optimised for mobile devices including iPhone and Android. The interface is touch-friendly with large tap targets. You can also install it as a PWA (Progressive Web App) for instant offline access directly from your home screen.
Is there a file size or usage limit? +
No limits at all. Compro is free to use with no file size cap, no daily limit, no account and no watermarks. The only practical limit is your device's RAM, which handles most photos easily.
Can I compress an image to a specific KB other than 50? +
Absolutely. Just change the number in the Target KB field to any size you need — 20 KB, 100 KB, 200 KB, 500 KB — and Compro will compress to that exact target. You can also switch to Quality % mode for manual quality control.
